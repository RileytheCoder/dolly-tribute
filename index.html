A few contributions Dolly Parton has done through The Dollywood foudation.

  • In 1988, The Dollywood Foundation was created in Sevierville, Tennessee, as a non-profit they began offering high school students scholarships.
  • In 1995, the Imagination library was created, it's purpose is to distribute books to children monthly till age five.
  • In 2016, The Dollywood Foudation created the My People Fund after wildfires destroyed parts of Tenessee.
  • In 2020, Dolly Parton personally made a one million dollar donation to the moderna COVID-19 vaccine.
