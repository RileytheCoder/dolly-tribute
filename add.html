A list of a few contributions Dolly Parton has done through The Dollywood foudation.

  • In 1988, The Dollywood Foundation was created in Sevierville, Tennessee, as a non-profit they began offering high school students scholarships.
  • In 1995, the Imagination library was created, it's purpose is to distribute books to children monthly till age five.
  • In 2016, The Dollywood Foudation created the My People Fund after wildfires destroyed parts of Tenessee.
  • In 2020, Dolly Parton personally made a one million dollar donation to the moderna COVID-19 vaccine.

The Dollywood Foundation and Scholarships

This foundation was originally created to offer scholarships to inspire children in Dolly's home town to achieve educational success. The focus was to lower dop out rates in the early 90s so Dolly promised every 7th and 8th grader she would personally gift them 500 dollars if they graduated highschool in her home county. This program helped lower the drop out rate from 35% to 6%. The Dolly foundation has also offered five $15,000 scholarships to senior students who successfully communicated their plan to realize their dreams.

The Imagination Library

The creation of this library was to help children learn to read. Books are still gifted to familys with children today since 1995, they mail high quality books for free no matter the familys income. This program was created to ship only to people in sevier county but has expanded greatly, here is a small time line...

  • 2002 – Native American Communities Added
  • 2006 – Imagination Library Opens In Canada
  • 2007 – Imagination Library Expands To United Kingdom
  • 2013 – Dolly’s Imagination Library Expands To Australia
  • 2019 – Dolly’s Imagination Library Expands To The Republic Of Ireland

    • With many more achievements in Dollywood's Imagination Library, they have some to accept many awards such as The Good Housekeeping Seal of Approval and Best Practices award from the Library of Congress Literacy Awards.

    My People Fund

    In 2016, the Great Smoky Mountains National Park was set on fire and is now know the 2016 Great Smoky Mountain wildfires, Dolly and the Dollywood companies came together and created the My People Fund to help protect the poeple of Sevier county. One thousand dollars was distributed to those who lost their homes in the fires for up to six months. The My People Fund ended in raising nine million dollars and assisted 900 families.

    COVID-19 Donation

    In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, Dolly Parton made a dontation of one million dollars to the Vanderbilt University Medical Center in 2020 and urged others to make donations too. Later the next year modernas version of the vaccine is considered 94.5% effective and she received the vaccination.
